Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 30, 2026

This privacy policy explains how The Digital Skatepark collects and uses information when you use the app.

Information We Collect

We collect account information, profile information, basketball session statistics, location attached to sessions or courts, uploaded profile images, highlight videos, audio included in highlights, posts, comments, and other app content you choose to create.

How We Use Information

We use this information to provide app functionality, including account access, basketball tracking, saved highlights, court activity, social features, profile pages, and account deletion.

Data Storage

App data may be stored locally on your device and on our backend services, including Supabase, when you sign in, sync, publish, or upload content.

Tracking

We do not use your data to track you across apps or websites owned by other companies. We do not sell your personal data.

Account Deletion

You can request account deletion from inside the app. Deleting your account removes your account and associated app data, subject to technical and legal limitations.
